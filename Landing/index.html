What BCFSleuth Offers

Professional BCF analysis with comprehensive support for all BCF versions and formats.

Lightning Fast Performance

Sub-2-second BCF processing with <200ms response times. Built for enterprise workflows with thousands of topics.

Universal BCF Support

Complete compatibility with BCF 2.0, 2.1, and 3.0 formats. Intelligent version detection with automatic field mapping.

Complete Data Privacy

100% client-side processing. Your BCF data never leaves your device. GDPR compliant with no server-side storage.

Professional Reports

Export to CSV, Excel, PDF, and Word formats. Complete coordinate extraction with 18 BCF coordinate fields.

Visual Analytics

Interactive charts with full-screen viewing. Custom analytics builder for detailed project insights and reporting.

Image Management

Complete image viewing with lightbox, zoom, and pan. Professional image reports with PDF layouts for documentation.

Simple, Powerful Workflow

Analyze BCF files in four simple steps. Professional results in minutes, not hours.

1

Upload BCF Files

Drag and drop BCF files or select from your device. Supports all BCF versions with automatic detection.

2

Select Fields & Filter

Choose from comprehensive field categories. Filter and sort data with advanced preview tables.

3

Analyze & Visualize

Generate interactive charts and analytics. Review images with professional lightbox viewer.

4

Export & Share

Export to multiple formats including CSV, Excel, PDF, and Word. Professional reports ready for clients.
